User data deletion

How to delete your SocialMediaLite account and the data we store about you on unwhelm.online.

Delete your account in the app (recommended)

  1. Sign in at unwhelm.online/login.
  2. Open Settings.
  3. Read the deletion warning, check the confirmation box, and type your username.
  4. Click Delete my account permanently.

Deletion is immediate and cannot be undone.

What is deleted

  • Your profile, banner, email, Facebook link fields, and login session.
  • All posts on your wall and all posts you authored.
  • Reactions, friendships, direct messages, and message folders.
  • Feedback you submitted.
  • Public syndication links for your posts.
  • Uploaded images tied to your account (profile assets, post photos, link-preview images).

What may remain

  • Comments you left on other people’s posts are replaced with (deleted user - deleted comment) so reply threads stay readable. The text of your comment is removed.
  • Server access logs may retain IP addresses for a limited time for security (not shown in the app).

Facebook data

Deleting your SocialMediaLite account removes data we stored from Facebook Login and import. To revoke the app’s access on Facebook’s side, open Facebook Settings → Apps and websites and remove ReadOnlyPosts / SocialMediaLite (whichever app name you connected).

Cannot sign in?

Use the feedback page and include the email or Facebook name on the account you want deleted. The site operator will verify ownership and delete the account manually.
