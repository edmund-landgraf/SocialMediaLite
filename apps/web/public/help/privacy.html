Privacy policy

SocialMediaLite (unwhelm.online) is a personal profile and friends messaging prototype. This policy describes what we collect, why, and your choices.

Last updated: June 6, 2026

Who operates this service

SocialMediaLite is operated by the site owner hosting unwhelm.online. Questions about this policy: use the feedback page (sign in optional for anonymous feedback).

What we collect

Account and profile

  • Username, display name, email — when you sign in (including via Facebook Login).
  • Profile photo URL — from Facebook Login or uploads you make on your profile.
  • Banner image — if you upload one on your own profile page.
  • Session cookie — to keep you signed in until you log out or the session expires.

Content you create

  • Posts, photo captions, comments, reactions, and messages between friends.
  • Friend connections and message folder names you create.
  • Feedback you submit on the feedback page.
  • Public syndication snapshots you explicitly create for a post (read-only public pages).

Facebook Login and timeline import

If you choose Login with Facebook, we use Meta’s Facebook Login with permissions limited to:

  • public_profile — name and profile picture.
  • email — email address associated with your Facebook account.

If you separately choose Connect Facebook for import, we also request user_posts so you can read your own Facebook timeline posts and copy selected ones into SocialMediaLite. We do not post to Facebook on your behalf from this permission. Import is user-initiated; we store the posts you import, not your entire Facebook account.

We store your Facebook user id and an access token in our database only as long as needed for login and import. We do not sell Facebook data to third parties.

Technical logs

  • Standard web server and application logs (IP address, request path, errors) for security and debugging.
  • Optional diagnostic logs for video playback and AI summary features when enabled on the server.

How we use data

  • Authenticate you and show your profile to you and your accepted friends.
  • Display posts, comments, messages, and syndication pages you create.
  • Import Facebook posts only when you explicitly request import.
  • Operate and secure the service (sessions, abuse prevention, troubleshooting).

We do not use your data for advertising profiles or sell personal information.

Who can see your information

  • Friends — content on your profile and direct messages between friends.
  • Public syndication links — only what you publish via “Create external link”; those pages are read-only and do not require a login.
  • Site operator — database and server access for hosting and support.

There is no global public feed in Phase 1; strangers cannot browse all users by default.

Storage and retention

Data is stored on servers controlled by the site operator (PostgreSQL database and local or configured file storage for images). We keep account data until you delete your account or ask for deletion (see data deletion instructions).

Your choices

  • Do not create an account — help pages and public syndication links do not require login to read.
  • Log out to end your session.
  • Delete your account at Settings (sign in required).
  • Revoke Facebook access in your Facebook app settings.

Children

SocialMediaLite is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
